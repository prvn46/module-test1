OUR MENUE
CHICKEN

½ kg CHICKEN (500 grams or 1 pound (preferably bone-in)) 2 to 4 tablespoons oil 1 cup onions (4 medium onions) (fine chopped) 1 to 2 green chilies slit (skip for less spicy) 1 tablespoon GINGER GARLIC PASTE or minced ginger garlic ½ cup tomatoes (2 medium tomatoes, pureed or finely chopped) ¼ cup yogurt (substitute with 12 cashews or 4 tbsps poppy seeds, refer notes) ½ to ¾ teaspoon salt (adjust as needed) ½ to 1 cup hot water (or coconut milk) 2 tbsp coriander leaves or mint leaves finely chopped
